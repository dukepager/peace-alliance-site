About Us

PEACE ALLIANCE
Home Care, LLC

Peace Alliance Home Care is a veteran-owned home care agency delivering elevated, client-centered care with a refined standard of excellence. We specialize in providing discreet, high-quality in-home care designed to support comfort, independence, and peace of mind—right where it matters most.

Our approach is rooted in professionalism, compassion, and attention to detail. We understand that true care goes beyond basic support—it's about creating a seamless, respectful experience for both clients and their families. From the first interaction to daily care delivery, we prioritize consistency, reliability, and a personalized touch that reflects each client's unique lifestyle and needs.

We proudly serve seniors, veterans, and individuals transitioning from hospital to home, including those recovering from surgery or managing ongoing health needs. Every care plan is thoughtfully tailored to ensure safety, dignity, and an enhanced quality of life.

As a veteran-owned organization, Peace Alliance Home Care is built on the core values of integrity, service, and commitment. These principles shape our culture, guide our caregivers, and define the exceptional level of care we provide.

At Peace Alliance Home Care, we don't just provide care—we deliver a higher standard of living at home.
