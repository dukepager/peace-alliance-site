Peace Alliance Home Care, LLC

Where Compassion Meets Professional Care

Peace Alliance Home Care was founded with a single mission: to provide families with peace of mind by delivering high-quality, compassionate in-home care. We believe every person deserves to live with dignity and independence in the place they call home. Our carefully screened and trained caregivers are committed to building trusting relationships with every client and family we serve.

Our Services

Tailored care for every individual need

🏠

Daily Living Assistance

Support with bathing, grooming, dressing, and everyday activities to help clients live comfortably at home.

🧠

Memory Care

Compassionate and patient care for those living with Alzheimer's, dementia, or other memory-related conditions.

🩺

Skilled Nursing

Professional nursing care in the home for those who need medical attention without leaving the comfort of their surroundings.

🤝

Companion Care

Meaningful companionship, conversation, and social engagement to support emotional wellbeing and reduce isolation.

🚗

Transportation

Safe, reliable transportation to medical appointments, errands, and social activities.

🍽️

Meal Preparation

Nutritious, home-cooked meals prepared with care to meet dietary needs and personal preferences.
